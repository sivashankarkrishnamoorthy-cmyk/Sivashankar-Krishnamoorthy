About My Work

I am a nanotechnology researcher and innovation leader with over 20 years of international experience transforming nanoscale ideas into technologies with global relevance. My work connects advanced materials, micro/nanofabrication, and biointerfaces to applications in electronics, photonics, and biomedicine.

My research journey spans nanooptics, biointerfaces, and scalable nanomanufacturing, with a focus on translating discovery into practical outcomes through multidisciplinary teams, industrial partnerships, and intellectual property.

This site highlights my research focus, leadership impact, publications, and invited engagements — with the aim of enabling new collaborations and shared innovation.
